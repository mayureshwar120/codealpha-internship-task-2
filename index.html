Our Story

Born from a love of craft & cacao

REFINES was born in 2017 from a single obsession: that a perfect cup of coffee and a piece of hand-tempered chocolate could completely transform a morning. Founders Aryan and Priya Mehta opened their first 400 sq ft space in Sector 18, Noida, driven by a belief that Delhi NCR deserved a café without compromise.

Today, REFINES operates two intimate spaces — our original Noida roastery and our Connaught Place gallery café in Delhi — each an homage to the unhurried pleasure of exceptional flavour.

Our Journey

A History of Refinement

2017

First REFINES opens in Sector 18, Noida — a 400 sq ft roastery. 7 menu items. Zero compromise.

2019

Chocolate atelier added. Bean-to-bar programme launches, partnering with Idukki cacao farmers.

2021

Connaught Place, Delhi. Gallery café, tasting room, and weekend brunch in the heart of the capital.

2024

Awarded Best Specialty Café in Delhi NCR by Food & Hospitality India. Online table ordering launches.
